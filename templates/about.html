About Face-Off

How to use Face-Off

1. Upload a photo you plan to upload to the internet to Face-Off.

2. Select ONE face.

3. Download the images when they are finished processing.

Note: Face-Off's face selection feature isn't visible on Safari yet. Please just select all faces if using Safari.

Privacy Policy

Your Data Privacy Is Important

- We permanently delete ALL of your photo data from our servers.

- We do NOT save your data in any form in our algorithms or models.

- Within 1 hour after uploading your images, all of your data is deleted.

- We DO save information about which selections you choose for statistics purposes, though this is anonymous data.

Session Cookies

- Cookies are used only for website functionality purposes and do not contain identifying information like location or IP address.
